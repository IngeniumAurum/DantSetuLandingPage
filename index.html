What DantSetu does

Everything a busy practice needs, nothing it doesn't

Purpose-built for dental clinics — from single practices to multi-clinic groups.

Smart Appointments

Schedule and track visits across every chair and clinic, with appointment details and prescriptions attached to each one.

Interactive Dental Chart

Plan treatment tooth-by-tooth on a visual chart, log procedures and follow each case from diagnosis to completion.
